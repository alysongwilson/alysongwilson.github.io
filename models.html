Linear Models, Hierarchical Models, and Model Checking

The learning objectives for this topic:
  • Derive and apply the Bayesian formulation of inference with a multivariate normal sampling distribution and for normal linear regression
  • Formulate and apply Bayesian hierarchical models
  • Create MCMC algorithms to make inferences for multivariate normal sampling distributions, normal linear regression, and hierarchical models
  • Implement posterior predictive checking, Bayesian sensitivity analysis, and Bayesian "goodness-of-fit" statistics
Readings: Hoff, Chapters 8-9; Albert, Chapters 7-9
