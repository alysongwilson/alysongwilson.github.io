Houston, Texas

The conference will be held on the campus of Rice University from 17-19 October with a two-day tutorial preceding on 15-16 October. The tutorial wil also be held on campus. To learn more about the Houston area, we suggest checking the visitor's bureau for Rice.

We have visited Rice several times, most recently in 2000. It is a great venue for a conference and this year's meeting with Rice as host promises to be a good one.
