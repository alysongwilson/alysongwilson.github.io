Local Arrangements

Travel to the Napa Valley Marriott:

Travel to Napa Valley Marriott can is approximately a 1 hour drive from either the San Francisco or Oakland airports. There is a link to directions from the hotel website. (Choosing an airport as the starting point is an option.)

Notes:
  • The government per diem for Napa is $125/$44
  • Transportation to the Napa Valley Marriott is available by rental car, cab or shuttle. Evans Airport Service, at Oakland or San Francisico, is one shuttle service recommended. The number is 707-255-1559. Shuttles depart according to a schedule and you will be booked on a shuttle close to your anticipated flight arrival time. At the time of this posting, the fee was $45 one way. You need to contact them 24 hours in advance. Otherwise, a full complement of rental cars are available.
  • The Napa Valley Marriott sales department will attempt to assist us if we surpass our room block by securing alternative lodging. Additional hotels in the immediate area may be accessed through the visitors site.
