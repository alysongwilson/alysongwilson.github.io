Washington, DC

CASD is being held at the the Bureau of Labor Statistics in Washington DC 20-24 October, with a two-day tutorial "Statistical Methods for Product Life Analysis and Accelerated Testing" on 20-21 October. The tutorial will also be held in the BLS facilities. The conference has visited the Washington DC area several times in its history, beginning with the inaugural conference in 1955 at the Diamond Ordnance Fuze Laboratories (now part of the Army Research Laboratory) in Adelphi MD and just last year at the American Statistical Association in Alexandria, VA. The DC area offers many advantages for our attendees.

The Bureau of Labor Statistics is in the Postal Square Building at 2 Massachusetts Ave., N.E. Washington, DC. More information about Alexandria, including hotels and restaurants can be found through the Visitor's Bureau site.
