Technical Program

The Conference on Applied Statistics in Defense (CASD) is a forum for the presentation and discussion of theoretical and applied papers related to the use of probability and statistics in solving problems in defense and national security. The conference provides an invaluable opportunity for interaction among academic, industry, and government scientists. It also serves a nurturing role in the elevation of statistical competence among researchers in other disciplines who find themselves statistical practitioners because of the compelling benefits statistical science brings to research, development, and testing.

The Conference on Applied Statistics in Defense will be held this year at the Bureau of Labor Statistics in Washington, DC. You can download a flyer for the conference here.
