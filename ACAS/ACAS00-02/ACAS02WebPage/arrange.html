Local Arrangements

Travel to Raleigh:

Travel to the Raleigh area is straight-forward. McKimmon Center Directions and Holiday Inn-Brownstone Directions are accessible through these links. The driving directions are repeated here.

To the Holiday Inn-Brownstone:

From I-40W to Wade Ave to 440S. Immediately exit to Hillsborough St. Left for approximately 2 miles. Hotel on right.

From I-40E to 440N to Hillsborough St. Left (east) 2 miles to hotel on right.

To the McKimmon Center:

From I-40 traveling east from the airport: Take Gorman Street Exit #295. Turn left onto Gorman Street. Go approximately 2 miles. The McKimmon Center is on the right approximately 1 mile past Avent Ferry Road, and just before Western Boulevard.

From I-40 traveling west: Take Gorman Street Exit #295. Turn right onto Gorman Street. Go approximately 2 miles. The McKimmon Center is on the right approximately 1 mile past Avent Ferry Road, and just before Western Boulevard.

Parking is plentiful around the McKimmon Center.
