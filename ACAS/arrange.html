Local Arrangements

Conference Facility:

The conference and preceding short course will take place at George Mason University in Fairfax, VA. There is a Mason Shuttle from Vienna Station Orange Line to Fairfax Campus. You can pick this up at the North Side of Vienna Station. The building used as our conference center is the HUB, building 56 on the map. It is a short walk from the Metro Bus stop on campus.

If driving, the Shendandoah Parking Garage is the closest to the HUB that has public parking. Most of the spots are reserved, but metered parking is available for visitors. It is located off of Patriot Circle on the East side of the campus. A walkway takes you directly to the HUB.  

Travel to and around Washington, DC:

The greater D.C. area is serviced by Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall, Dulles International, and Ronald Reagan Washington National airports.
