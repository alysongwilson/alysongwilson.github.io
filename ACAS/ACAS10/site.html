Cary, North Carolina

The conference is being hosted by SAS at the Cary site from 20-22 October with a two-day tutorial preceding on 18-19 October. The tutorial will also be held on the SAS campus. Successful ACAS meetings have been held twice in the Raleigh/Durham area in the past ten years, and we are optimistic about the meeting planned for this year.

To learn more about the Cary/Raleigh area and siteseeing opportunities, we suggest checking Visit Raleigh. It is a very nice area with a great deal to do. There are good shopping opportunities, top knotch museums (the Natural Sciences Museum is wonderful), a wide array of dining opportunites, evening entertainment, and more. Check out their calendar for activities that interest you and your family!
