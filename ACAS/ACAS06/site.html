Durham, North Carolina

The conference will be held at the Radisson in Research Triangle Park, from 18-20 October 2006 with a two-day tutorial preceding on 16-17 October held at NISS. To learn more about the Raleigh/Durham area, we suggest the visitor's bureau for Raleigh or the visitor's bureau for Durham.

We last visited North Carolina in 2002 when NC State and ARO were hosts. It is a great venue for a conference and this year's meeting with NISS as host promises to be a good one.
