Local Arrangements

Conference Facility:

All events related to the conference will be held at the Georgia Tech Hotel and Conference Center.

Travel to the GA Tech Hotel and Conference Center:

Travel to GA Tech Hotel and Conference Center is straightforward by auto, plane, or train. Directions are available on the hotel web site.

If flying into Atlanta, there are some options to consider from the Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport. Taxis and a full complement of rental cars are available. If you choose a rental car, be aware that at the hotel there is a $15/day parking fee. A better option might be the Atlanta Link, which will provide a round trip shuttle to and from the hotel for $32. Perhaps better still is the Metropolitan Atlanta Rapid Transit Authority (MARTA) system, which for $1.75 one-way will take you to the Midtown MARTA Transit Station. From there, the yellow GA Tech Trolley will take you for free directly to the hotel. This last option will take approximately 30 minutes.

In and around Atlanta, the free GA Tech Trolley combined with MARTA will allow you to travel the city.
