Local Arrangements

Conference Facility:

The conference will take place on the grounds of the Naval Postgraduate School. The location for other conference events (Army Wilks Award Banquet and Social) have not as yet been determined. The Hyatt Regency Monterey is the conference hotel is located across the street from a convenient gate to NPS.

Travel to the Hyatt Regency Monterey:

Directions to the Hyatt are available on the hotel web site. Directions are available from the nearby Monterey Penninsula Airport and from points north if you prefer to fly into San Francisco or San Jose and drive down.

In an around Monterey many popular locations are within walking distance. The conference facilities are also a short walk away. Limited NPS parking will be available with a parking pass. Details will come later.
