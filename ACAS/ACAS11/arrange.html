Local Arrangements

Conference Facility:

The conference and preceding short course will take place at the O'Callaghan Annapolis Hotel, in Annapolis MD.

Travel to Annapolis:

The airport servicing Annapolis is Baltimore Washington International (BWI). Please see their web site for ground transportation choices. The Annapolis Hotel is approximately 1/2 hour from the airport.

Driving to the hotel from most directions will involve US-301/US-50. If traveling from the north, west, or south, you will look for US-301 N/US-50 E toward the Annapolis Bay Bridge. Take exit 24 to merge onto MD-70 S/Rowe Blvd toward Annapolis S. Turn right onto Taylor Ave. At the traffic circle, take the 3rd exit onto West Street. 174 West Street will be on your left.

Travel in Annapolis:

The O'Callaghan is less than a mile from shopping, historic Annapolis, and the Naval Academy, easily within walking distance. However, for the Social, the conference has made arrangements with eCruisers of Annapolis, a small electric car shuttle. Payment is in tips only. A VIP phone number for travel to an from the Social will be provided by the conference to ensure priority pickup. 

Parking:

  • There is parking available at the hotel for $18/day. 
  • There is parking available at Knighton Garage at the intersection of West St. and Colonial Ave. right across from the hotel for just $5/day.
