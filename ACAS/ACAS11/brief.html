Program in Brief

The program outline appears below. We welcome technical papers discussing methods or applications with a potential tie to defense and security issues. More details on the conference will follow. Check http://www.armyconference.org for official updates.

  • Welcome:

    Gary O. Fowler, Department of Mathematics, United States Naval Academy

  • Short Course:

    Applied Logistic Regression
    A short course on logistic regression offered by COL Rodney Sturdivant (United States Military Academy and Ohio State). The course will be based on material from the soon to be published Hosmer, Lemeshow, and Sturdivant update of the classic text on this topic.
