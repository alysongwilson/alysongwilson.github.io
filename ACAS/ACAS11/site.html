Annapolis, Maryland

The conference is being held at the O'Callaghan Annapolis Hotel from 19-21 October, with a two-day tutorial on Logistic Regression preceding on 17-18 October. The tutorial will also be held in the O'Callaghan. This is the first visit of the conference to historic Annapolis, MD, and its proximity to Washington, D.C. and the vast DoD enterprise in the region makes us optimistic about the meeting planned for this year.

Annapolis, briefly our nation's capital, played an important role in our colonial history. The O'Callaghan has convenient parking, is within easy walking distance of St. Johns College, the Naval Academy, the State House, and the Annapolis waterfront and shops.To learn more about the Annapolis area and sightseeing opportunities, we suggest checking Visit Annapolis.
