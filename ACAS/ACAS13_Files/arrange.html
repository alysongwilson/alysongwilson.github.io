Local Arrangements

Conference Facility:

The conference and preceding short course will take place at the American Statistical Association in Alexandria.

Travel to and around Alexandria, VA:

The greater D.C. area is serviced by Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall, Dulles International, and Ronald Reagan Washington National airports. The ASA is a short walk from the Braddock Road Metro Station. There are several hotels in the area. See the Visitor's Bureau for details.  

Public Transportation: 

The various public transportation choices in and around the Capital Region are the VRE from Virginia, Amtrak, Metro, and MARC.

Parking:

The Visitor's Center also has information as to parking in the area.

Lodging:

There is no conference hotel for this meeting. Attendees may choose from the many Alexandria hotel options. 

    Note:
    • The current government per diem for Alexandria is $184 lodging / $105 meals.
