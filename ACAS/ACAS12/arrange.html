Local Arrangements

Conference Facility:

The conference and preceding short course will take place at the Casa Munras Hotel & Spa in Monterey, CA.

Travel to Monterey:

The airport servicing Monterey is Monterey Regional Airport (MRY). Please see their web site for ground transportation choices. The Casa Munras is approximately 10 minutes from the airport. Some will choose to fly to San Francisco or San Jose. It is a bit of a drive, but the scenery coming down the coastal Rt 1 is worth doing if you have extra time.

Travel in Monterey:

The Casa Munras is less than 1/2 mile from shopping, historic Monterey, easily within walking distance. The Monterey-Bay Aquarium is about a 30 minute walk. Other sites are accessible by car or bike. Bikes are available for rental at the hotel.

Parking:

There is free parking available for hotel guests.
