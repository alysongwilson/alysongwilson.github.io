Tempe, Arizona

The conference will be held on the campus of the Arizona State University from 21-23 October with a two-day tutorial preceding on 19-20 October. The tutorial will also be held on campus. To learn more about the Tempe area and siteseeing opportunities, we suggest checking the Visitor's and Convention Bureau for Tempe or Tempe in Touch.

To see what is beyond the Tempe area we suggest visiting the Arizona Guide.

This is the first time the conference has been held in Tempe. We are pleased that ASU was willing to host this year's meeting.
