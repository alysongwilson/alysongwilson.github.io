ABSTRACT

In Part 2 of this panel discussion, we ask questions of our panel of prominent experts from diverse areas of industry, government, and academia about the changing roles for statisticians in the SE workplace and discuss some of the opportunities and challenges for the future. In this second article we consider:
  1. Emerging roles for statisticians with SE.
  2. How universities can get involved with speeding the development of new statisticians.
  3. Key first steps to establishing SE as a field.
  4. Potential pitfalls in the development of SE.
  5. How national statistics organizations can help.
  6. How individual statisticians can get involved.
