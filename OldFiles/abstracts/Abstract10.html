ABSTRACT

A special section contains a collection of articles on how statisticians collaborate with other scientists. Keller-McNulty, Wilson, and Wilson describre their experience at Los Alamos National Laboratory, followed by comments from Karr (NISS); Landwehr, Mallows, and Tendick (Avaya Labs); Nair (U-MI); Stufken (U-GA); and Ghosh-Dattar and Paddock (RAND) who draw on their vast experience as collaborating statisticians in industry or at national agencies such as NISS and the NSF.
