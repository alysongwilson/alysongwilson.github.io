Multiparameter Inference

The learning objectives for this topic:
  • Define: marginal posterior distribution, conditional posterior distribution
  • Know three ways to calculate a marginal posterior distribution from a joint posterior distribution
  • Find the marginal posterior distributions and conditional posterior distributions for a normal likelihood with noninformative and conjugate prior istributions
  • Find the marginal posterior distributions for a multinomial likelihood with various prior distributions
  • Use the brute force, inverse cdf, rejection sampling, and weighted bootstrap/SIR methods to generate random samples
Reading: Hoff, Chapters 4-5; Albert, Chapters 4-5
